# Launch Readiness

Use this mode pack with `mako-loop`. Read `../../references/launch-readiness.md` before judging or preparing a launch.

## Core Rule

Launch only the promise that is actually proven. Cut or hide anything that makes the product feel broader than the working workflow.

## Required Proof

- Clear positioning and buyer.
- Demo path works.
- Screenshots or live URL are verified.
- Pricing/waitlist/signup/contact path is clear.
- Known gaps are hidden, framed honestly, or deferred.
