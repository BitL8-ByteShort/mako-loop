# Example: Launch Readiness

Prompt:

```text
Use MakoLoop with launch-readiness. Review this product like a buyer, a Product Hunt visitor, and a skeptical engineer. Find anything that would make the product feel unfinished, unsafe, unclear, slow, broken, or hard to trust. Fix what is in scope, verify the buyer-facing workflow, and give a final ship/no-ship decision.
```

Launch checklist:

- Clear product promise.
- One obvious primary action.
- Working onboarding or first-value path.
- No visible placeholder text or diagnostic filler.
- Responsive layout.
- Error, loading, and empty states.
- Trust/security signals.
- Pricing or next-step clarity when applicable.
- Build/test/browser proof.
- Security review for sensitive surfaces.
