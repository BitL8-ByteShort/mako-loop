# Example: Research And Recreate An App

Prompt:

```text
Use MakoLoop with app-research-recreate and implementation-grade-research. Research an app like Wispr Flow or MacWhisper and produce enough implementation-grade research to build an original dictation app for normal MacBooks using a local model/runtime. Do not stop at inspiration. Produce architecture, data flows, model/runtime decision, acceptance criteria, and verification.
```

Expected outputs:

- Current source list with dates.
- Product promise and target user.
- Workflow and screen map.
- Core technical architecture.
- Model/runtime decision.
- Privacy and local-processing constraints.
- Implementation sequence.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Security and database review scope if persistence is included.
- Verification matrix.

Boundary:

Compete on the workflow and outcome. Do not copy protected branding, private APIs, proprietary copy, or paid/account-only material.
